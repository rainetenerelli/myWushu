myWushu YOUR WUSHU COMMUNITY BUILDING ASSISTANT

CREATED BY RAINE TENERELLI AND ELANEY CHENG

After a year and a half of online classes, some students rejoined their campus communities feeling isolated and found it difficult to engage.
For CS 220, two students are ready to tackle this issue for their student organization through the power of human-centered design.
We created a prototype for a mobile app called myWushu to help.
