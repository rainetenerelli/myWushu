introduction.

Hello there!
We are the Mood Managers: Michaella, Julie, and Eugenia, a team of dedicated CS 220 students ready to tackle modern issues through human-centered design. We created a prototype for a mobile app called Serene.

Problem Statement:
At the beginning of the U.S spread of the COVID pandemic in mid-March, college students were suddenly evacuated from their campuses and physically displaced. Later, protests broke out regarding police racially-based brutality and violence, further spurring feelings of anxiety and a lack of security in the world. Through online interviews and user analysis, we gathered that many college students in the summer of 2020 were feeling isolated and exhibiting frequent low moods.

Our solution:
Our application would aim to help users cope with emotional turmoil by combining features of commonly used mood-tracking apps as well as other emotional supports in order to create a single mobile destination for feeling better.
