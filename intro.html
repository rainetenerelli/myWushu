introduction

Hello there!
Our names are Raine and Elaney, we are seniors at Wellesley College in CS 220 who are who are passionate about tackling modern issues through human-centered design. We created a prototype for a mobile app called myWushu.

Problem Statement:
We have both been members of Wellesley Wushu, a martial arts organization, since our first year at Wellesley. Over the COVID-19 pandemic, Wushu experienced a startling drop off rate during remote practices and after we came back to Wellesley many of our teammates mentioned that the transition from virtual to in person was difficiult, socially and emotionally. By conducting interviews with several members of the team, we noticed that people who had difficulty persisting with Wushu lacked the feeling of a strong sense of community.

Our solution:
MyWushu aims to build the mental/emotional resilience of Wellesley Wushu by offering four features to facilitate more virtual and in person interaction between members. In doing so we hope to increase practice and event attendance, allow members to get to know each other, build a sense of community, and increase teammate interaction.
